Career Options in Science

Biologist Chemist Physicist

Biologist

Biologists study living organisms and their interactions with each other and their environments. Career options include:

  • Research Scientist
  • Ecologist
  • Microbiologist
  • Zoologist

Chemist

Chemists study the composition, structure, and properties of matter. Career options include:

  • Analytical Chemist
  • Organic Chemist
  • Pharmaceutical Chemist
  • Materials Scientist

Physicist

Physicists study the fundamental properties of matter and energy. Career options include:

  • Astrophysicist
  • Quantum Physicist
  • Medical Physicist
  • Meteorologist
